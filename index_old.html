Services

 

I provide a host of one-off and project-based freelance technical and marketing writing services. Generally speaking, if you need to write about your product or your industry at large, I'm your man. But there's oftentimes more to the challenges that led to the need you're facing than just banging out a piece of documentation.

Scaling software companies isn't easy in an agile world. Documentation is always an after thought, yet a lack of strategy and foresight in both capturing and retaining key knowledge at an early stage can be toxic as an org scales.

In my experience as a marketing writer, technical writer, and product manager at various start-ups turned venture-backed and even public, I've come to understand what it takes to set a business up for success. Now, I help companies to introduce, refine, and manage frameworks and tools for enterprise-caliber knowledge management, intra- and extra-company communications, technical writing, and product marketing.
