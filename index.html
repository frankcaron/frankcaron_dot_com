Aboot

My name is Frank Caron, and I am a product guy and seasoned writer with a love of both software and writing.

I like to describe myself as a scribe, curator, organizer, and collaborator. In another life, I was probably a biographer in ancient Rome who had a contempt for both literature and his subjects.

Currently, I live and work in Toronto. I've previously lived and worked in Waterloo and San Francisco.

I come from a background in app and web development. This experience has been complemented over the years with a passion for information architecture, paritcularly in the areas of user interface and product copy.
